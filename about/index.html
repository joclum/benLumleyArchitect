ABOUT

In 2005 I established as a sole practitioner, and have built a strong reputation transforming residential properties with an emphasis on functionality, economy and design. During this time I have been able to rely nearly entirely on recommendations from satisfied past clients, and am committed to delivering a high quality, personalised service.

In addition to residential projects, I’ve also designed workspaces, recreation centres, and community spaces. Every design is informed by the surrounding environment, my clients, and the people that will use the space.
